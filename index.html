Fangqi Zhao

Communication & Human–AI Interaction · Health Communication · Digital Equity

PhD Applicant (2026) Mixed Methods AI & Empathy Telehealth

I investigate how emerging technologies shape psychological and social mechanisms during information seeking, with a focus on empathy, algorithmic bias, and digital equity. My work spans AI-enabled telehealth, human–AI collaboration, and recommender systems, aiming to design transparent, user-centered tools that improve trust and access for underserved populations.

Research Interests

Human-Centered Design for Underserved Populations

Designing AI systems that balance human-centered, efficiency, and Sustainability, studying manipulative AI, situation-awareness, and user trust.

Health Communication & Equity

Exploring AI-assisted telehealth recruitment, CBPR messaging, and interventions for neurodivergent women (e.g., ADHD).

Algorithmic Fairness

Bias detection and mitigation in recommender systems, with outcomes for anxiety, cyberchondria, and help‑seeking.

Human‑AI Teams

Situation awareness, advice‑taking thresholds under conflict, and measurable frameworks for sustainable use.
