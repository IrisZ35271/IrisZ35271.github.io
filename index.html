Fangqi Zhao

Communication & Human–AI Interaction · Health Communication · Digital Equity

PhD Applicant (2025) Mixed Methods AI & Empathy Telehealth

I study how emerging technologies shape information seeking and well-being in health contexts, with a focus on empathy, equity, and human–AI collaboration. I aim to design transparent, user-centered tools that measurably improve access and trust for underserved communities.

Research Interests

Human–AI Interaction & Empathy

How chatbot empathy, transparency, and recommendation design influence autonomy, trust, and long‑term engagement.

Health Communication & Equity

Identity‑concordant outreach, community co‑design (CBPR), and low‑burden digital touchpoints to improve access and dignity.

Algorithmic Fairness

Bias detection and mitigation in recommender systems, with outcomes for anxiety, cyberchondria, and help‑seeking.

Human‑AI Teams

Situation awareness, advice‑taking thresholds under conflict, and measurable frameworks for sustainable use.
